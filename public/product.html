Typo tolerance

Normally, customers doesn't know exactly the name of what they're searching for. With Apisearch, no matter if they type wrong the word that they are looking for, the system will take care of this.

Multi-language search

Apisearch work with many different languages. Once you're creating the data index, you cat set the language you want to work with.

For more information check it out in the documentation site.

Geo distributed

Your data can be organized by its location. So you can easily build dynamic map searches, location inputs or aggregate the results by the place where they come from.

Check out the docs to see how this can be done.

Unlimited filters and aggregations

Allow your customers to refine their searches by color, rating, brand, type or whatever attribute you want. It's fully customizable and does't compromise the search performance.
